1. The State Of Art

GPS is no more bizarre to us. It plays a pivotal role in helping us to locate the specific streets and areas almost everywhere. It tends to be said that GPS is one of the most amazing innovations that humans have created. So, what precisely is GPS? We can understand that GPS (global positioning system), is a satellite-based route framework consisting of an organization of 24 satellites circling the earth consistently. It resembles a watchman flying around the earth and has a mission of guarding like a camera. Even though there is a variety of versions, they work as a whole and not individually. Moreover, the way GPS works are quite simple: For example, there are three fixed points on the map, GPS will measure the distance between you and each point; then it will draw three circles with the radius equal to the distance, and finally, the point at which the three circles meet is where you are. In that way, GPS can almost identify your exact location. There is no limitation of this system, GPS works in every single climate condition, anyplace on the planet, 24 hours per day. The most famous and familiar application using GPS is definitely Google Maps. We use this app on a daily basis to help us in finding ways to a destination. It identifies our current location, anticipates the time is taken and the distance, tracks our route, and guides us to the address given.
